SOLUTION

Removing Comments Section

As many celebrities, especially musicians and artists, suffer from keyboard warriors, removing comments section for the entertainment news reduce the amount of malicous comments on the Internet. It is not a perfect solution as the keyboard warriors will keep posting aggressively on any other platforms that has comments section like YouTube and Instagram. Also, the issue is with the keyboard warriors, so there should be additional effort to deal with them.


Internet Real-Name System

With this, people will not be able to make accounts without entering their real name, then their identities will not be fully anonymous. Keyboard warriors mostly post aggressively on the internet since no one knows who they are on the internet. However, with the Internet Real-Name System, the keyboard warriors will post less malicous comments.


Harsher Punishment

Many of the keyboard warriors post aggressively, thinking they will not get caught and even if they get caught, the punishment is not severe. In fact, it is hard to catch all the keyboard warriors and to The punishment is not that harsh comparing to how much others suffered from their actions. With a harsher punishment, there will definitely be less keyboard warriors.


Artifical Intelligence

An artificial intelligence (AI) that filters malicious comments and does not allow those comments to be posted will definitely beneficial. With such AI, the keyboard warriors will not be able to post aggressively at all. It will keep the Internet clean.
